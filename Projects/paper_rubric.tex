	\begin{tabular}{||m{.95in}||m{1.5in}|m{1.5in}|m{1.5in}||}
		\hline\hline
		&\textbf{Great (4-5 points)} &\textbf{Adequate (3 points)}&\textbf{Poor (1-2 points)} \\\hline\hline
		
		\textbf{Abstract} &The abstract is well written and clearly pertains to the paper.&There is a small disconnect between the abstract and paper.& The abstract does not relate to the paper.\\\hline
		
		\textbf{Introduction} &The introduction gives a background for the reader and how the information relates to the course.&The introduction provides some information and is moderately interesting.&The introduction provided little or no relevant information.\\\hline
		
		\textbf{Relation to Course}&It is clear how the content of the paper relates to the content of the course.&It is somewhat unclear how the paper relates to the course.&There is little to no relation between the topic and linear algebra.\\\hline
		
		\textbf{Mechanics}&There are little to no spelling or grammatical errors.&There are some spelling or grammatical errors.&The paper is riddled with spelling and/or grammatical errors.\\\hline
		
		\textbf{\LaTeX}&The typesetting of the paper is perfect.&There are some things missing with the typesetting.&The typesetting makes the paper hard to read.\\\hline
		
		\textbf{Proof}&The minimum of 1 required proof is well written, relates to the topic and uses definitions or theorems, with reference, from the course.&The proof either does not totally pertain to the course or to the paper/topic.&The proof is poorly written and does not pertain to the topic of the paper.\\\hline
			
		\textbf{Example} &The minimum of 1 example illustrates the topic of the paper and is clear.&The example either does not illustrate the topic of the paper or is somewhat difficult to understand.&The example does not pertain to the topic and is poorly explained.\\\hline
		
		\textbf{Sources} &There are at least two sources outside of the textbook and computational software. The sources are also properly cited.&There is only one extra source or the sources are poorly cited.&There are no other sources.\\\hline\hline
	\end{tabular}
